Our StoryAuthentic South Indian Cuisine Since Generations

Woodlands Restaurant is one of Udupi's well-known vegetarian restaurants, serving authentic South Indian cuisine prepared using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes. Founded with a passion for preserving the culinary heritage of South India, we offer a comfortable family-friendly dining experience with delicious food, quick service, and quality hospitality.

Every dish at Woodlands is crafted with care, honoring the traditional cooking methods and flavor profiles that have made South Indian cuisine beloved across generations. We source the finest ingredients and prepare each meal with the dedication and love it deserves.

Our commitment to purity—both in our vegetarian philosophy and in our ingredients—ensures that every plate that leaves our kitchen is a testament to quality, authenticity, and the warmth of South Indian hospitality.
