Our Story

A Legacy of Warmth & Hospitality

Hotel Rays Inn was born from a vision to create a space where guests feel the warmth of home combined with the luxury of a premium hotel. Located at the serene Guruvayankere Junction in Belthangady Taluk, just a stone's throw from the sacred Dharmastala temple, our hotel has become a beloved destination for pilgrims, business travelers, and families alike.

What started as a modest boarding facility has evolved into a full-service hospitality destination, featuring 14 beautifully appointed guest rooms, the celebrated Pegs multi-cuisine restaurant, and the grand VELJIL party and conference hall with a capacity of over 400 guests.

Our commitment to excellence in service, comfort, and cuisine has earned us the trust and loyalty of thousands of guests who return to us time and again.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide exceptional hospitality experiences that blend modern luxury with traditional warmth. We strive to make every guest feel valued, every meal memorable, and every event extraordinary — setting the standard for hospitality excellence in the Dharmastala region.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the most trusted and beloved hospitality destination in Karnataka's temple circuit. We envision a future where Hotel Rays Inn is synonymous with premium comfort, authentic cuisine, and celebrations that create lasting memories for generations.

Tourism Around Dharmastala

Discover the rich cultural heritage and natural beauty surrounding Hotel Rays Inn.

Dharmasthala Temple

The revered Sri Manjunatheshwara Temple, one of Karnataka's most visited pilgrimage sites, offering divine peace and spiritual solace.

5 km away

Bahubali Statue

The 39-foot monolithic statue of Lord Bahubali (Gommateshwara), a magnificent Jain monument of spiritual significance.

5 km away

Manjusha Museum

A fascinating museum housing a diverse collection of artifacts, vintage automobiles, paintings, and cultural treasures.

6 km away

Netravathi River

The serene Netravathi River flows nearby, offering picturesque views and peaceful riverside walks for nature lovers.

3 km away

Charmadi Ghat

A breathtaking mountain pass through the Western Ghats with stunning views, luxury scenery, and thrilling hairpin bends.

30 km away

Kukke Subramanya

Famous temple town dedicated to Lord Subramanya, nestled in the foothills of Kumara Parvatha in the Western Ghats.

50 km away
