Our StoryAuthentic South Indian Cuisine Since Generations

Woodlands Restaurant is one of Udupi's well-known vegetarian restaurants, serving authentic South Indian cuisine prepared using fresh ingredients and traditional recipes. We offer a comfortable family-friendly dining experience, delicious food, quick service, and quality hospitality.

Every dish is crafted with care, honoring the culinary traditions of Udupi while maintaining the highest standards of food quality and customer service. Our family welcomes yours to experience the warmth of traditional South Indian dining.
